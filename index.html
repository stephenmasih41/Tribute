Nikola Tesla

The man known for his inventions

Here's a time line of Nikola Tesla:

  • 1856 - Born in Smiljan, Austria
  • 1887 - Alternating Current Power Systems
  • 1897 - The Development of Radio
  • 1891 - The Tesla Coil
  • 1893 - Fluorescent and Neon Lighting
  • 1895 - The Hydroelectric Power Plant at Niagara Falls
  • 1898 - Remote Control
  • 1909 - Tesla Turbine
  • 1930 - The "Teleforce"
  • 1943 - Died at the age of 86
